The Imagined Order

The Imagined Order is the secret agency that controls Saucy, and they live underneath Saucy.



Interdimensional Portal

Interdimensional portal is an instant portal to any dimension or reality by being shot out of a portal gun to reach any universe imaginable.


​

The Bridge

​The bridge is a hub that connects every reality to ever exist. It is a tunnel that connects everything together. Also known as the in-between or the nexus.


Time Warp

Time warp is where the IO experiments with the gaps between the past and the future, with the present in the middle. Time warping is being truly lost in time.


Zero Point

The zero point is the core of reality. It can be stable and interact with other realities via interdimensional connections, or destabilize time while unstable.
